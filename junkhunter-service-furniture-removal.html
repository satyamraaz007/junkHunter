Furniture Removal

WHY OUR JUNK FURNITURE REMOVAL?

Junk Hunter is your one-stop shop for all furniture removal services. From residential furniture such as dressers, bed frames, and entertainment centers to commercial furniture like computer desks and chairs, we take it all. Since we’ve got so many team members ready to help, we’ve always got enough muscle to go around, too. So count on us to remove furniture from your property safely. We don’t drop anything, and we’re even fully insured, so give yourself some peace of mind with that knowledge!

Whether you need mattress removal or wardrobe removal, you can always trust us to provide you with an honest price for our work. At Junk Hunter, we use volume-based pricing, so you’ll pay for the amount of truck space your furniture fills up and not a penny more. We never add hidden fees to your final bill, either. After all, who likes those? No one!

OUR FURNITURE HAULING PROCESS
  1. We will arrive on time for your appointment with an empty truck and a team that’s ready to haul. Accept our upfront quote, and we’ll start hauling your furniture right away.
  2. Furniture can be heavy, but that won’t stop our team. We’ll have multiple team members gently lead the furniture through your property and out the door.
  3. We’ll load the furniture up into our truck, head back, and sweep up behind ourselves. Like our results? Then we’ll accept your payment!
  4. We are an eco-friendly furniture removal company, so if you have any gently used furniture, we’ll be sure to donate it to a nearby Habitat ReStore.
