Mattress Recycling

An average mattress weighs 65 pounds and occupies 23 cubic feet of space in a landfill. They have a compaction rate 400% less than traditional garbage, which means they take up much more landfill space relative to their size. Moreover, mattresses can cause significant issues with landfill equipment and have a tendency to rise to the surface ("float") over time.
